# 4. Training Interference Prompt

### Explain the interference effect between endurance training and strength training.

### Objective

To understand how endurance training (such as running) may affect muscle development.

### Result

The AI identified the following mechanisms:

- endurance training activates the **AMPK pathway**
- resistance training activates the **mTOR pathway**

These pathways may compete depending on training intensity and volume.

Suggested strategies to reduce interference:

- perform strength training before endurance sessions.
- separate training sessions by several hours.
- control total training volume.

---

# 5. Practical Application Prompt

### Create an 8-week 5k running plan for someone who also performs strength training.


### Objective

To generate a practical example of **hybrid training**, combining:

- endurance running.
- resistance training.

### Result

The AI generated a progressive **8-week running plan** designed to gradually increase total running time, starting from approximately **15–20 minutes per session** and progressing to **40–50 minutes in later weeks**.

### Generated Running Plan

| Phase | Weeks | Running Structure (3 sessions per week) |
|------|------|------|
| Adaptation | Weeks 1–2 | Warm-up (5 min walking) + 5x (1 min light run / 1 min walk) + 5 min cool-down |
| Initial Volume | Weeks 3–4 | Warm-up + 3–5x (2 min running / 1 min walking). The goal is to reduce walking time and increase running time |
| Consolidation | Weeks 5–6 | Introduce 1 interval session (example: 3x 1000m fast with 2 min rest) and 2 continuous runs of 15–20 minutes |
| Final Phase | Weeks 7–8 | Continuous runs of 25–30 minutes (or reaching the full 5km distance). In week 8, reduce training volume (taper) to allow proper recovery before the goal run |

### Insight

This training structure allows runners to progressively build endurance while maintaining compatibility with strength training routines.

### Objective

To understand the nutritional factors involved in body recomposition.

### Result

The analysis highlighted:

- protein intake recommendations between **1.6 and 2.2 g/kg of body weight**.
- carbohydrates as the primary fuel source for high-intensity exercise.
- the importance of caloric balance and nutrient timing.

# Troubleshooting and Prompt Refinement

During the NotebookLM exploration process, some challenges were identified:

- overly broad prompts produced generic responses.
- unclear context reduced the relevance of answers.
- complex prompts sometimes required simplification.

To improve results, the following strategies were used:

- breaking complex questions into smaller prompts.
- asking more specific and contextual questions.
- refining prompts iteratively.

The prompt engineering process demonstrated that:

- iterative prompting leads to deeper insights.
